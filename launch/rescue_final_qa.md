# BW-83A Rescue Final QA

Rescue has been reviewed after the BW-82 polish run.

Covered behavior:
- Remember Why can be added or edited directly inside Rescue.
- Open your why scrolls to the Remember Why card.
- Next Right Action supports built-in actions plus custom Other capture.
- Calm Reset runs three rounds with a short settle pause after exhale.
- Calm Reset clears the settle state before returning to inhale.
- Rescue cards support button navigation and horizontal swipe navigation.
- Wave Completion saves a Victory without forcing the user back Home.
- Wave saved follow-up offers Open Log, Return Home, and Stay in Rescue.
- Still strong keeps the user inside Rescue and offers next-step support.
- Slip saves without shame language and opens Support.

Manual APK checks:
1. Add/edit Remember Why inside Rescue.
2. Tap Open your why from Next Right Action.
3. Select Other and save a custom next action.
4. Run Calm Reset through all three rounds.
5. Swipe Rescue cards left and right.
6. Complete wave and confirm Wave saved follow-up.
7. Open Log from Wave saved follow-up.
8. Mark Still strong and confirm Rescue follow-up remains visible.
9. Mark I slipped and confirm Support opens.
