# BreakWave — BW-70B Screen Privacy Shield

## Purpose

BW-70B adds an optional Screen Privacy Shield to reduce casual exposure of sensitive recovery content.

## Behavior

When enabled, BreakWave asks Android to block normal screenshots and screen recordings while the app is open.

## User-facing safety language

This reduces casual exposure but is not absolute protection. Someone could still capture the screen with another device.

## Release note

The setting is optional so internal testing, Play Store screenshots, and support screenshots remain possible when the shield is off.
