# BreakWave — Screenshot Visual QA Checklist

## Purpose

BW-60 prepares BreakWave for screenshot-ready internal review before public Play Store launch.

## Main tab checks

### Home

- Branded BreakWave header appears at the top.
- Fast urge entry is obvious without scrolling.
- Home does not show repeated empty-state cards before the user has data.
- Prototype copy such as “being shaped into” is not visible.
- Bottom navigation does not cover important buttons.

### Rescue

- Branded BreakWave header appears at the top.
- Intensity and Remember Why appear early.
- Timer, calm reset, rescue cards, redirect actions, completion, and support flow in a sensible order.
- “Open Support” copy appears instead of unfinished placeholder wording.

### Log

- Branded BreakWave header appears at the top.
- Recent entries and new log form are readable.
- Buttons clear the bottom navigation.

### Support

- Branded BreakWave header appears at the top.
- Support is grouped into clear sections.
- BreakWave Plus is visible and understandable.
- Admin/prototype wording is not visible to normal users.

## Plus checks

- BreakWave Plus explains free versus paid clearly.
- Plus does not claim real billing is live before Play Billing setup.
- Pricing direction is visible.
- Paid value feels specific, not vague.

## Final visual bar

BreakWave should feel calm, organized, private, and intentionally branded before public launch.
