# BreakWave — CBT-Informed Recovery Foundation v1

## Purpose

BW-62 makes BreakWave explicitly CBT-informed while keeping the product language safe.

BreakWave should not claim to provide therapy, diagnosis, medical advice, emergency care, or a cure.

## Safe product description

BreakWave uses CBT-informed recovery tools to help users identify triggers, interrupt urges, reframe thoughts, choose healthier actions, and build a relapse-prevention plan.

## User-facing promise

BreakWave helps you interrupt urges, understand your triggers, build healthier responses, and stay connected to your reasons for recovery — one moment at a time.

## Core behavior-change model

Trigger → Thought → Urge → Action → Consequence → Better Plan

## Product principle

BreakWave should help users not only stop porn use, but learn how to handle stress, loneliness, boredom, shame, anger, and temptation without escaping into another destructive pattern.

## Required MVP / near-MVP features

1. Trigger → Thought → Urge → Action log
2. Rescue thought-challenge card
3. Healthy replacement action selector
4. Warning against replacing porn with other harmful habits
5. Slip review without shame
6. “When to seek professional help” support screen
7. Clear disclaimer: BreakWave is a support tool, not a replacement for therapy

## Disclaimer

BreakWave is a support tool. It does not provide therapy, diagnosis, medical advice, emergency care, or a cure, and it is not a replacement for a licensed professional.

## Free versus Plus direction

Free core:
- urgent Rescue
- basic CBT-informed interruption
- basic log
- slip review without shame
- professional-help guidance
- harmful replacement warning

BreakWave Plus:
- deeper relapse-prevention plan
- longer CBT-informed history
- trigger/thought pattern insights
- guided routines by emotion
- accountability templates
- premium Christian depth paths
- exports and longer-term planning tools
