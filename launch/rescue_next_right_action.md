# BreakWave — BW-65 Rescue Next Right Action

## Purpose

BW-65 makes Rescue more action-oriented by asking the user to choose one clear next right action before marking the wave complete.

## Core flow

Urge hits → open Rescue → calm down → choose one healthy replacement action → mark the wave interrupted.

## MVP decision

The next right action selector is single-select.

## Reason

During a high-pressure urge, the user needs one clear move, not a long checklist.

## Included actions

- Put the phone down
- Leave the room
- Open your why
- Text someone safe
- Cold water reset
- Take a short walk
- Pray for one minute, when Christian mode is active

## Harmful replacement warning

Rescue reminds the user not to replace the urge with another harmful habit.

## MVP safety copy

Rescue should remind users: do not replace the urge with another harmful habit.
